Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 12, 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you register and use Spendly, we collect information such as your name, email address, and the financial data you choose to track (expenses, amounts, categories, and dates).

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to provide you with insights into your spending habits. We use the information to display your dashboard, generate summaries, and enable the core functionality of the Spendly application.

3. Data Storage

We securely store your data using industry-standard practices. However, you acknowledge that no system is entirely foolproof. We regularly back up data to prevent loss in the event of hardware or system failure.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may occasionally use third-party analytics services to help us understand how users interact with our app, but this data is anonymized and aggregated.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us at privacy@spendly.example.com.
